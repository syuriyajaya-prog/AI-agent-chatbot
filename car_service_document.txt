MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE

Engine Oil:
Conventional oil: Every 5,000 km or 3 months
Semi-synthetic oil: Every 7,500 km or 6 months
Full synthetic oil: Every 10,000 km or 12 months

Tyre Rotation: Every 10,000 km
Wheel Alignment: Every 20,000 km or if vehicle pulls to one side
Wheel Balancing: Every 20,000 km or after tyre replacement

Air Filter: Every 15,000 km or 12 months
Cabin Air Filter: Every 15,000 km or 12 months
Spark Plugs (standard): Every 30,000 km
Spark Plugs (iridium): Every 80,000 km

Brake Pads (front): Every 40,000 km or when thickness below 3mm
Brake Pads (rear): Every 60,000 km or when thickness below 2mm
Brake Fluid: Every 2 years regardless of mileage

Coolant Flush: Every 40,000 km or 2 years
Transmission Fluid (auto): Every 40,000 km
Transmission Fluid (manual): Every 50,000 km
Power Steering Fluid: Every 50,000 km or 3 years

Timing Belt: Every 60,000 km (critical - engine damage if neglected)
Timing Chain: Inspect every 100,000 km
Battery: Typically lasts 2-4 years, test annually after year 2

CANCELLATION & RESCHEDULING

Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled online through your AutoCare account.

Cancellation policy:
More than 24 hours before appointment: Free cancellation
Between 12-24 hours before appointment: RM 20 late cancellation fee
Less than 12 hours before appointment: RM 50 no-show fee
No-show without cancellation: RM 50 fee charged, account flagged

Rescheduling:
Free rescheduling up to 2 times per booking
Subject to slot availability
Must be done at least 6 hours before original appointment

VEHICLE DROP-OFF & COLLECTION

Drop-off: Arrive 10 minutes before your slot.
Collection: Customer will be notified via SMS and email when vehicle is ready.
After-hours collection: Available at designated secure parking with pin code access.
Vehicle storage: Free for first 24 hours after completion. RM 30/day thereafter.
Courtesy car: Available for Premium Package customers (subject to availability, booking required).

WARRANTY ON PARTS & LABOUR

All genuine OEM parts: 12 months or 20,000 km warranty (whichever comes first)
Aftermarket parts: 6 months or 10,000 km warranty
Labour warranty: 3 months on all services performed
Battery replacement: 24 months warranty (pro-rated)
Tyre replacement: As per tyre manufacturer warranty

Warranty is void if:
Vehicle is serviced elsewhere after AutoCare service
Vehicle is involved in an accident
Improper use or modification detected
